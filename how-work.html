How Shanli Work

Gebhaly is an Egyptian registered company that provides online shopping services.

Each step guides you to the next

1 Do the Shopping

From the Homepage, click on the “Stores” tab above to find out US stores or go direct to your desired one. Click on the store you want to purchase your item from. Take your full tour to compare and pick up your item.

2 Put in Your Order

After settling on the product you want, copy its link and return to Gebhaly page to paste the link in the blank box.

3 Fill in the Product Details

Once you paste the link, you will find the details of your product automatically written down. Just take a look to review and enter any missing/needed data, such as color, size, and quantity, and then click on “Add to Cart” button.

4 Int. Shipping

After clicking on the “Checkout” tab, you have to choose your International Shipping type (whether to calculate it in Cairo Office or we review the order and send you full quotation) and then click on “Proceed to Checkout” tab. Hence, you need to register to undergo the process.

5 Choose Payment Method

On the sidebar, have a final look at the total cost, including the total online price, customs, VAT, Gebhaly fees, and international and local shipping, and confirm the Coupon Code, if you entered, affected the process. Then choose your payment method and tick the “Terms and Conditions” and “International Shipping fees” to proceed to payment.

6 Receive Your Order

After your product reaches our office in USA, our shipping company will bring it to Cairo. The process of delivering will be undertaken between 7 and 14 working days since the product is delivered to our office in USA.
